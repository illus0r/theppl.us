Name: Caleb Clark

Talk Topic: Resurrection: A Sustainable Fashion Show
Title: Designer

Organization: Enemy to Fashion 

Website

 

See Caleb on The PPL’s stage:
Monday, September 3, 2012 from 7:00PM – 9:00PM

Enemy To Fashion is a timeless modern brand that was created in 2005 with a meager bank account, thousands of ideas and hundreds of years of fashion history. E2F roots itself in classic Americana, taking cues from our ancestors by using lasting, vintage fabrics tied together with modern accents to create an authentic style. All pieces are handmade with energy and boldness from products often found at flea markets, estate sales and even junk piles. Creators at E2F find that drawing styles from their grandfathers, fabrics from our history, and visions from their creativity can and will inspire their garments and bring a uniquely modern but rooted approach to fashion.
