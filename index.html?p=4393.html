DFA Live Studio at The PPL

Posted by Desiree Kane on Sep 2, 2012 | 0 comments

Home » Blog » DFA Live Studio at The PPL

Last week in Tampa, Republicans hoped to change the conversation going into November. But as you saw, what occurred was a conversation with an empty chair  about how the United States got into this recession.

We’re teaming up with Democracy for America this week in Charlotte to provide a live studio where progressive leaders, pundits, and your favorite bloggers and reporters will join us for conversations. You’ll hear from folks like Rep. Keith Ellison, Rep. Donna Edwards, Sen. Sheldon Whitehouse, Sen. Jeff Merkley, Van Jones, Lizz Winstead, The Nation’s Ari Berman, Gov. Howard Dean and more.

We know not everyone can make it to Charlotte, so we’re live-streaming these conversations online! All talks will be streamed Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. EST.

You’ll find the stream below, and we’ll update this post with a broadcast schedule when it’s available!
